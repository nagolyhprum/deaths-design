Task summary:

- Cover four facings with three walk-cycle frames per facing.

Implementation notes:
- Asset path: `assets/characters/main_character/main_character_walk_spritesheet.png`
- Layout: 4 rows x 3 columns, with each row representing one facing and each column representing a walk frame.
- Facing order: down-right, down-left, up-left, up-right.
- Frame size target: 96 x 96 pixels to leave enough room for readable silhouette and foot placement.

Art direction:
- Young adult male silhouette with short dark hair, a muted hoodie or jacket, dark pants, and bright shoes for foot readability.
- Emphasize shoulder angle, hip rotation, and alternating foot placement so the walk cycle stays legible in an isometric camera.
- Favor clean shapes and contrast over fine detail because the game tone depends on fast retries and instant hazard readability.
